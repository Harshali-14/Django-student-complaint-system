Our Mission

ResolveX aims to provide a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform for managing student complaints. Our mission is to ensure that every concern is recorded, tracked, and resolved fairly while improving communication between students and administration.

What We Do

ResolveX simplifies the complaint management process by enabling users to submit complaints online, monitor their status in real time, and receive updates throughout the resolution process. The system helps administrators manage and resolve issues efficiently.

Key Features

✔ Online Complaint Registration
✔ Real-Time Complaint Tracking
✔ Department & Category-Based Management
✔ Secure User Authentication
✔ Admin Dashboard for Monitoring
✔ Complaint Status Updates
✔ User Profile Management
✔ Responsive and User-Friendly Interface
